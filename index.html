The Jewell Assessment

Your AI strategy has a blind spot.

Most AI readiness checklists stop at infrastructure and data. They never measure the thing that actually determines outcomes — taste. The judgment to know what to deploy, when to hold back, and whether your organization is ready to execute. This assessment measures it.

For executives evaluating AI strategy. For technical leads navigating implementation. For anyone trying to understand where their organization actually stands.

What We Keep Seeing

Foundation

$200K AI pilot. Dead at month three. The cause: 40% of their claims data was locked in scanned PDFs nobody knew about. The AI worked. The data wasn't there.

Architecture

92% accurate demand forecasting model. Useless. The procurement workflow it fed required three levels of human approval. By the time anyone acted, the market had moved.

Accountability

40-page AI governance policy. Board-approved. Nobody's read it. When audited: 60% of data pipelines had zero lineage tracking. The policy couldn't describe what it was supposed to govern.

These aren't edge cases. They're the pattern.

Assessment

The Jewell Assessment

An adaptive, conversational diagnostic that evaluates your organization's AI readiness across five layers — and reveals your strategic judgment through scenario-based choices, not self-reporting.

The output isn't a score. It's a constraint diagnosis: which specific layer will undermine your next AI initiative if you don't address it, and three specific actions for the next 90 days.

5–12 minutes. No login required. Results are immediate.
